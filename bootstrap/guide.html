Questions and answers

Factions are powerful alliances that define how you fight, grow, and conquer. Each faction represents a unique philosophy of war, shaping your heroes’ abilities, preferred combat style, and path to dominance. Choosing a faction isn’t just cosmetic—it determines how you approach battles, defend your territory, and crush your rivals.

Ironbound Legion

Masters of steel and raw power. This faction thrives in close combat, wielding swords, shields, and heavy armor. Their heroes hit hard, endure longer battles, and dominate the front lines through sheer physical strength.

Arcanum Covenant

Wielders of devastating magic. Focused on spells, elemental attacks, and battlefield control, this faction overwhelms enemies from a distance. Their magic can turn the tide of battle—but timing and positioning are everything.

Shadowborn Clans

Swift, cunning, and lethal. Specializing in speed, precision, and ambush tactics, Shadowborn fighters strike fast and disappear just as quickly. They excel at disrupting enemy formations and stealing territory before opponents can react.

Power is earned on the battlefield.

You level up by:

  • Winning battles against monsters and rival players
  • Conquering territories to expand your influence on the world map
  • Collecting resources from claimed land
  • Upgrading cities, structures, and heroes to unlock stronger abilities and bonuses

Every conquered region fuels your rise, unlocking new units, skills, and strategic options as your legend grows.

Victory comes from strategy—not just strength.

  • Choose the right heroes for each battle
  • Counter enemy tactics—steel can overpower fragile mages, while magic can devastate heavily armored units
  • Position wisely, using terrain and formations to your advantage
  • Upgrade abilities to unlock devastating attacks and passive bonuses

Whether you’re clashing blades up close or raining destruction from afar, understanding the balance between swords, magic, and tactics is the key to domination.
